About the series

Set in a world divided into four nations, Avatar: The Last Airbender follows the journey of Aang, the last Airbender and the Avatar, as he strives to bring peace and balance by mastering all four elements and defeating the Fire Nation.

Brought to life by Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko, this series has captured hearts worldwide with its deep themes, humor, and stunning animation.
